On Empty Spaces

One might question if this violates the "interesting content" rule. I'm not so sure, but there was a large blank space on my homepage, and I felt like I had to fill it up with something. So here you have it: a paragraph of meaningless ramble intended to fill up the empty space left behind by having a picture that is just too large. A smart person might reduce the size of the image, or even put up a second picture to compensate for the first. But not me. Because I started writing before I started thinking and now I reap what I sow.
